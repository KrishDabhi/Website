What can we help you with?

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the operating hours for buses and trains?

Our buses and trains operate from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily. Check the Schedules page for details.

How can I purchase tickets or passes?

Tickets can be purchased online, via the mobile app, at ticket counters, or vending machines. Visit the Tickets & Passes page for more information.

Are there discounts for students, seniors, or disabled passengers?

Yes, discounts are available for students, seniors, and passengers with disabilities. Visit the Discounts & Eligibility page for details.

How do I plan my trip?

Use the Trip Planner Tool to map your route, including start point, destination, and travel time.

What should I do if I lose something on a bus or train?

Fill out our Lost and Found Form or call our customer service hotline at 1-800-TRANSPORT.

Are bikes or pets allowed on buses and trains?

Bikes are allowed on trains and some buses. Pets in carriers are welcome; service animals are always permitted.

What measures are taken for safety and cleanliness?

We ensure daily cleaning, CCTV surveillance, and real-time alerts for passenger safety.

How do I report an issue or file a complaint?

Use our Feedback Form, call us at 1-800-TRANSPORT, or visit a customer service center.

Is the public transportation system accessible for people with disabilities?

Yes, all facilities include wheelchair ramps, priority seating, and elevators for accessibility.

How do I stay updated about delays or service changes?

Subscribe to alerts, download the Transit App, or follow us on social media for updates.
